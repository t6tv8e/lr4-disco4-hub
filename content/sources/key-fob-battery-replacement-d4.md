## Summary

A quick walkthrough showing how to replace the battery in a Discovery 4 key fob. The dashboard will display a warning message like "key fob battery required" when the battery is low. The process involves releasing the hidden mechanical key blade, opening the battery tray, swapping the coin cell battery, and reassembling.

## Key Points

- Press the top tab to release the mechanical key blade from the fob
- Use the smooth side (not the textured side) to access the battery compartment -- it opens like a small door
- Pull the battery tray out from the top
- Keep the fob with the Land Rover logo facing down so the battery stays seated in its tray
- Match the plus (+) symbol on the battery to the plus (+) marked on the plastic tray
- The tray has a groove at the bottom that aligns with a block in the aperture, preventing incorrect insertion
- Touching the battery with clean hands is fine despite some advice to the contrary
- After reassembly, the tray clicks shut and you should test lock/unlock to confirm

## Parts Mentioned

- CR2032 coin cell battery (standard key fob battery, not explicitly named but standard for D4 fobs)

- This is a very simple procedure requiring no tools
