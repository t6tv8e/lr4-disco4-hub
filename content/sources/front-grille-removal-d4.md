## Summary

A very short and straightforward video showing how to remove and refit the front grille on a Discovery 4. The process requires no tools — just four clips at the top and two prong/docking-station locators at the bottom. The presenters note that the D4 grille removal is slightly different from the Discovery 3 method.

## Key Points

- Open the bonnet first
- Push down the four clips along the top of the grille
- Place hands under the bottom of the grille and give a sharp pull outward
- The bottom of the grille has small prongs that locate into plastic docking stations on the body
- To refit: line up the top clips first, push to engage them, then push the bottom to locate the two lower prongs
- No tools are required for this job

## Parts Mentioned

- Front grille (clip-on, no screws or bolts)

## Notes

- The process is noted as being slightly different from the Discovery 3
- This is one of the simplest jobs on the D4 front end and is often a prerequisite for other work such as bumper removal or headlight access
