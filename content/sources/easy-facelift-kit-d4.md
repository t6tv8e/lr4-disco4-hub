## Summary

This video walks through a complete front-end facelift conversion to make an early (pre-facelift) Discovery 4 look like the later facelift model, without requiring any paintwork or body shop visits. The presenter explains the key visual differences between early and late D4s: round vs square fog light surrounds, orange-accented headlights vs blacked-out headlights with signature LED strip, and the different grille shape (straighter on early, more curved on late).

The kit approach uses a custom injection-moulded filler strip to bridge the gap between the late grille and the early bumper, plus clip-on "fake" fog light bezels that replicate the recessed late-model look. The headlights are plug-and-play but must match the existing type (halogen to halogen, xenon to xenon). The entire conversion can be completed in a Saturday morning with basic tools.

## Key Points

- Early D4 has round fog lights, orange-accented headlights, and a straighter grille shape; late D4 has squarer fog surrounds, blacked-out headlights with LED signature strip, and a more curved grille
- Headlights are plug-and-play with no wiring changes needed, but you must match halogen-to-halogen or xenon-to-xenon
- If upgrading from halogen to xenon headlights, software changes are required using an IID tool
- Xenon headlights have AFS (Adaptive Front-lighting System); the bottom ridge is more pronounced on newer units, making them a tight fit over the early bumper lip
- Simply fitting the late grille leaves a visible gap along the bumper — the custom filler strip is essential
- Filler strip is injection moulded in gloss black; it can be painted to match body colour at much lower cost than repainting the whole bumper
- Filler strip is secured with self-tapping screws through 3mm pilot holes; no wiring runs beneath the mounting area
- Fog light bezels are a two-piece design attached with 3M double-sided tape; the outer positioning tab goes in first due to the slope
- Clean the bonding surface with an alcohol wipe before applying the adhesive fog bezels
- Grille removal: push down tabs at the top, pull out firmly at the bottom
