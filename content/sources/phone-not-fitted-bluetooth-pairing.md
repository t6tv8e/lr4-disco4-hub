## Summary

A short video addressing the confusing "phone not fitted" message that appears on the Discovery 4's infotainment screen when you press the phone button. Despite the wording, this message does not mean the phone hardware is missing from the vehicle -- it simply means no phone has been paired via Bluetooth. The video walks through the quick process of pairing a phone to resolve the message.

The fix is straightforward: go into the phone's Bluetooth settings, turn Bluetooth on, look for the hands-free device in the available devices list, select it, and enter a PIN code (2121 is used in the video). Once paired, the "phone not fitted" message disappears and the phone system works normally.

## Key Points

- "Phone not fitted" on the D4 infotainment screen means "phone not paired" -- the hardware is present
- To pair: turn ignition on, go to phone's Bluetooth settings, enable Bluetooth, find the hands-free device, tap to pair
- PIN code used: 2121 (may accept any PIN)
- Once paired, the phone system becomes fully functional
- Access the phone screen from the Home button on the infotainment system
