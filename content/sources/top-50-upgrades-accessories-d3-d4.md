## Summary

A walkthrough of approximately 50 aftermarket upgrades and accessories fitted to a 2005 Discovery 3 project car with 150,000 miles. The presenter walks around the entire vehicle covering exterior styling, interior trim, lighting, wheels, brakes, and various cosmetic enhancements. Many of the accessories are designed to give the Discovery 3 a more modern Discovery 4 facelift appearance.

While focused primarily on the Discovery 3, many of these accessories are cross-compatible or relevant to the Discovery 4, and the video is tagged for both models. The presenter notes that fitting videos for all accessories are available on their YouTube channel, and product links are provided on their website.

## Key Points

**Exterior:**
- Headlight guards and indicator de-orange sticker kit (to look like Discovery 5 / facelift)
- Colour-coded washer jet covers (factory grey plastics degrade under UV)
- Fog light bezels available in chrome, carbon effect, and body colour
- D4-facelift-style front grille — a direct swap that transforms the look in 10 minutes
- Lettering change from "Land Rover" to "Discovery" (available in chrome, red, matte black, gloss black)
- Chrome number plate surrounds
- Snorkel for deeper wading
- LED sweeping side repeaters (Audi-style), mirror covers with LED puddle lights
- Wind deflectors (smoked)
- Stainless steel window trim covers (protects rubber trim from bulging/blistering)
- Colour-coded door handle covers and chrome scratch plates behind handles
- Body-colour wheel arches (D3 has textured plastic; D4-style colour-coded arches available)
- Mud flaps
- Anodised aluminium fuel filler cap (red or blue)
- D4-style rear lights (most popular upgrade) — bolt straight on with resistor to prevent fast flashing
- LED reverse light bulbs for much better visibility
- Rear lettering, HSE Luxury badge in gloss black
- Factory-style tread plate with rubber steps
- Optional dummy roof aerial (Range Rover Sport style)
- Side steps and stainless steel side bars available

**Interior:**
- Gloss black door grab handles (replacements for worn originals, available in gloss black and chrome)
- Black and silver instrument badges (from supercharged Range Rover Sport)
- Drilled aluminium foot pedals and footrest (Range Rover Sport style)
- Gloss black dash fascia panel (as fitted to HSE models)
- Gloss black centre console trim
- Gloss black gear change surround
- Upgraded steering wheel with perforated leather and ergonomic grip
- Piano black or wood-finish switch pads
- Full LED interior light upgrade
- LED welcome/puddle lights
- Controllable pedal lights (phone app controlled)
- SV Autobiography armrest knobs (chrome, replaces black plastic)
- Chrome handbrake switch (as fitted to D4)
- Gloss black cubby box lid

**Wheels & Brakes:**
- 21-inch Discovery 4 alloy wheels fitted to D3
- Stainless steel capped wheel nuts
- Black and chrome centre caps
- Drilled and grooved brake discs
