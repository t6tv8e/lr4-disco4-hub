## Summary

This video covers diagnosing an air suspension fault on a Land Rover Discovery 3 (also applicable to the Discovery 4). The vehicle displays a "Suspension fault - Normal height only" error message after the compressor fails to reach pressure within the allowed time window. The presenter first attempts the cheapest possible fix -- replacing the air suspension compressor relay -- before moving on to consider compressor replacement.

The relay (position R7 in the engine bay fuse box next to the battery) is a simple plug-in component that can only be inserted one way. Unfortunately, in this case, replacing the relay did not resolve the fault, indicating the compressor itself had failed. The video notes the compressor is located behind a plastic cover at the rear of the vehicle near the rear wheel.
