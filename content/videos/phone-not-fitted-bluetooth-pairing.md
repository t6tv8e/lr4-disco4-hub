## Summary

A short video addressing the confusing "phone not fitted" message that appears on the Discovery 4's infotainment screen when you press the phone button. Despite the wording, this message does not mean the phone hardware is missing from the vehicle — it simply means no phone has been paired via Bluetooth. The fix is straightforward: go into the phone's Bluetooth settings, turn Bluetooth on, find the hands-free device, select it, and enter a PIN code (2121). Once paired, the message disappears and the phone system works normally.
