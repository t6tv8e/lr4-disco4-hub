## Summary

Peter replaces the factory door-mounted turn signals on his Discovery 4 with aftermarket sequential LED units purchased from Amazon for around 30 euros. The entire swap requires no tools — just fingers — and takes only a few minutes per side. The new units feature a smoked housing and a sequential sweep animation that significantly modernizes the vehicle's appearance.
