## Summary

Peter reviews his BFGoodrich KO2 All-Terrain tyres (265/65 R18) after 25,000 miles of mixed driving on his diesel Discovery 4. The tyres excel off-road with reinforced sidewalls over 1 cm thick and impressive tread longevity (7-8 mm remaining), but come with trade-offs: a 1.5-2 L/100km fuel economy penalty and noticeably degraded wet-road grip after two years as the rubber hardens. At 250-300 euros per tyre in Europe, they are expensive but Peter considers them worth the price and plans to repurchase.
