Peter walks through replacing the H7 headlight bulbs on his Discovery 4, taking advantage of the vehicle's fully clip-based grille and headlight assembly design that requires no tools. He installs Osram Night Breaker 150%+ bulbs to replace the yellowed and blackening originals, but finds them not white enough, prompting him to order Philips H7 LED bulbs (road-legal in Austria and Germany) for a follow-up video.
