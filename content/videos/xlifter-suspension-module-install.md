## Summary

Peter installs the XLifter plug-and-play air suspension module on his Discovery 4, demonstrating the straightforward process of removing two driver's side trim panels, intercepting three connectors on the factory suspension ECU, and powering the control unit via the cigarette lighter. The module extends the factory lift range to +75 mm (Sky Rocket mode), overrides the 40 km/h speed limit in raised modes, and adds features like auto-leveling for camping, trailer assist, and compressor temperature monitoring. Calibration completes automatically in seconds on a flat surface.
