## Summary

Peter performs a DIY transfer case oil change on his Discovery 4, a 15-30 minute job that had never been done on this vehicle based on the lack of service records. The dramatically black old fluid compared to honey-colored fresh oil in a side-by-side cup test made a compelling visual case for why this commonly neglected service is critical. The procedure requires only an 8mm hex key, 1.5 liters of ATC oil, and a hand pump, with the transfer case easily accessible on the driver side without removing any underbody panels.
