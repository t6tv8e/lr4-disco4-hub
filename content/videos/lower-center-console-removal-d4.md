## Summary

A quick walkthrough of removing the lower centre console (around the gear selector area) on a Discovery 4. The presenters are doing this to search for a factory-fitted cable used for retrofitting a reversing camera, though they note their particular vehicle doesn't have the cable. The procedure involves removing the cup holder insert, two self-tapping cross-head screws, the gear knob, and then lifting the whole console assembly up and away.

The video is relatively short and straightforward. One important tip is to disconnect the battery before unplugging the various connectors underneath, as the vehicle can get upset about things like the handbrake being disconnected. The presenters also show the gear change cable, handbrake release cable, and other components visible once the console is removed.
