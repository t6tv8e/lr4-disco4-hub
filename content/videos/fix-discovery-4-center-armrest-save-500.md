## Summary

Peter replaces the broken center console armrest handle on his Discovery 4 after discovering that Land Rover only sells the complete armrest assembly for 500-600 EUR. He sources a near-identical aftermarket handle from AliExpress for approximately 1 EUR and walks through the full disassembly, spring transfer, and reassembly process using T40 and TX20 Torx bits. The result is a functional repair at a fraction of the dealer cost, with the only trade-off being a slightly glossier chrome finish compared to the original brushed aluminum.
