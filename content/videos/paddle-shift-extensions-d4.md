## Summary

A short video showing how to fit die-cast aluminium paddle shift extensions to a Discovery 4. This is the final step in a multi-part paddle shift upgrade series. The extensions are a cosmetic and ergonomic upgrade that stick onto the existing paddles using adhesive, making them larger and easier to reach. The same extensions also fit the Range Rover Evoque, Range Rover Sport, and L322 Range Rover with paddle shifts.
