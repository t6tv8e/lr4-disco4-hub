## Summary

Peter installs a 110W Lensun Solar panel on his Discovery 4 hood using a protective decal underlayer that prevents paint damage and makes the installation reversible. The kit includes an IP67 waterproof charge controller with Bluetooth app monitoring, but the 3M double-sided tape for panel mounting must be purchased separately. The key practical tips are mixing isopropanol into the soapy water for easier decal application, maintaining minimum 10-degree temperatures, and having a second person for accurate panel placement.
