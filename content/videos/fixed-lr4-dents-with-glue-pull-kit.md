## Summary

Peter demonstrates how to remove dents from his Discovery 4 using a glue pull repair kit purchased on Amazon for approximately 90 EUR. The process involves applying heated glue tabs to the dent, pulling with a lifter tool, and repeating the cycle 10-15 times per dent -- a time-intensive but accessible technique for amateurs. He achieves a significantly improved result after about 11 pull cycles, though notes that sharp body creases remain difficult without interior access tools, and the key trade-off is patience rather than skill.
