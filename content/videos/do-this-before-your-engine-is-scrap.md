## Summary

Peter addresses two common Discovery 4 diesel complaints -- cold-start jerking and engine nailing noise -- with two low-cost interventions. A Liqui Moly diesel additive eliminated the winter jerking within 400 km by cleaning dirty injectors, while switching from the Land Rover recommended 5W-30 to a Castrol Magnetic 5W-40 oil noticeably reduced cold-start engine noise without increasing fuel consumption. Both products are presented as affordable, low-risk maintenance strategies for high-mileage diesel engines.
