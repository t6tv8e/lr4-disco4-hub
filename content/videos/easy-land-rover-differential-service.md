## Summary

Peter walks through a front and rear differential oil change on his Discovery 4, choosing 75W-140 oil over the Land Rover recommended 75W-90 for better temperature performance. The front differential drain plug was visibly coated in metallic particles, making a strong visual case for changing this fluid more frequently than Land Rover's generous 240,000 km interval. The job requires basic tools (8mm hex key, 38mm oil key, hand pump) and covers both the standard open rear differential and notes for the Electronic Torque Managed variant.
