## Summary

Peter demonstrates a DIY transmission fluid change on his Range Rover Evoque's 9-speed automatic, extracting approximately 4 of the total 6.5 liters. The most valuable discovery is the counterintuitive technique of raising the car higher (rather than keeping it level) to drain significantly more fluid, repeating the lift cycle multiple times. While this video features the Evoque, Peter notes his Discovery 4's transmission fluid was much worse -- black like motor oil -- making regular partial changes an important preventive strategy for both vehicles.
