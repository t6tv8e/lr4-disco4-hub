## Summary

Peter replaces his Discovery 4's worn rock sliders, which required annual rust restoration due to stone damage from BFGoodrich off-road tires, with new MP4x4 units. The new sliders feature a sealed rear cap that prevents dirt ingress -- a design improvement he had not seen on other rock sliders. Key practical advice includes dry-fitting before final install, jet-washing and treating underbody rust first, and greasing bolts before mounting.
