## Summary

Peter replaces his Discovery 4's rear ladder with a compact MP4x4 unit costing 160 euros -- less than 35% of the equivalent Front Runner ladder at 470 euros. The swap was necessary because the original full-size ladder crashed into the Rhino Rack batwing awning when opening the trunk. The install involves removing severely rusted T30 screws (one required drilling out), disconnecting the camera cable, and applying foam tape to protect paintwork before mounting the new stainless-hardware ladder.
