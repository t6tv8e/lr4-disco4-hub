## Summary

Christian and Vera drive 13 hours from Frankfurt to Barcelona, drop off their son Phillip at a BMX park, and spend multiple days overlanding through Northern Spain and the Pyrenees foothills in winter. They visit abandoned ghost towns (deserted in the 1960s-70s when villages lost electricity and farmland to dam flooding), a dinosaur fossil site, and hike the "Little Grand Canyon of Spain" — a 15 km round trip featuring the "Stairway of Death," a suspension bridge, and trail sections carved into rock walls, compared favourably to Angels Landing with zero crowds. Night temperatures of -5 degrees C are handled by the rooftop diesel heater, which sustains overnight operation on a 4-5 year old Goal Zero with 40% charge remaining. Route finding relies on Pistes & Cols road books with downloadable GPS tracks. The drone was lost on a later day when flown while driving. Total trip: approximately 4,000 km and 450 litres of diesel.
