## Summary

Peter replaces both rear brake hoses on his Discovery 4 after a viewer spotted a dangerous bulge in a previous video. He uses Delphi Technologies replacement hoses at approximately 20 EUR each, walks through the removal and installation process using 11mm and 8mm spanners, and then bleeds all four brake corners using a one-man bleeding bottle. Since the brake fluid was over three years old, he also performs a complete fluid flush, expelling approximately 750ml of old DOT 4 fluid and replacing it with fresh fluid as recommended by Land Rover.
