## Summary

This video walks through a complete engine oil and filter change on the Land Rover Discovery 4 with the 3.0 TDV6/SDV6 engine. The presenter demonstrates removing the sump plug (which is a plastic hose-type fitting rather than a traditional sump plug), draining the oil, and then locating and replacing the oil filter canister buried in the V of the engine.

The oil filter housing requires a 32mm socket to remove, and the video covers removing the old filter element, cleaning the housing, fitting the new O-ring and filter, and refilling with oil. The presenter notes that the D4 TDV6 does not have a traditional dipstick, so oil level must be checked via the onboard computer system. Approximate oil capacity is cited as 5.9 litres, though they advise checking the specific capacity for your vehicle.
