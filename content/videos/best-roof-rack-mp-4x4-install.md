## Summary

Peter installs an MP 4x4 aluminium roof rack on his Discovery 4, choosing it over Front Runner after his previous Front Runner rack developed rust from its steel screws. The aluminium rack with stainless hardware costs around 1,000 euros and carries 400 kg static / 200 kg dynamic, but the crossbars lack pre-drilled holes for flexible accessory mounting -- a notable limitation compared to Front Runner's design that Peter suggests MP 4x4 should address.
