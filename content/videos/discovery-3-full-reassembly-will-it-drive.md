## Summary

Christian and Vera reassemble a 1,000 EUR Discovery 3 nicknamed "Sir Rescue" for their friend Robin after six months of rebuilding work. The video documents the full road-legalisation process including clutch and brake bleeding, driveshaft installation, and complete drivetrain fluid changes — all of which had likely never been done in the vehicle's 165,000 km life. The transfer case fluid contained water, the manual transmission fluid was near-black, and the rear differential drain plug was seized. Critical torque specifications are provided throughout. A dangerous test drive incident revealed all four wheels had insufficiently torqued lug nuts, causing shaking at highway speeds. The vehicle was ultimately registered and road legal, though suspension height calibration and brake pedal issues remain for follow-up.
