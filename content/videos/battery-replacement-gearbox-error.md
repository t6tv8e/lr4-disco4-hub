## Summary

Peter replaces the main battery on his Discovery 4 after three gearbox error warnings in two weeks, which turned out to be caused by insufficient voltage from a depleted battery rather than an actual gearbox fault. The video covers the physical swap (Banner to Varta), the essential IID Tool battery registration procedure via Service Test > PCM Body Control > Battery Replacement, and both the manual and IID Tool-assisted methods for relearning window calibration after a battery disconnect.
