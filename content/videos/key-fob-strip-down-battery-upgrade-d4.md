## Summary

This video demonstrates a full strip-down of a Discovery 4 key fob and shows how to transplant the circuit board from the original early-style key case into a newer, sleeker Evoque-style key case. The presenters were responding to a viewer who had been quoted around 770 GBP by a dealer for a replacement key fob.

The circuit board from the early D4 key does fit the later Evoque-style case, but one corner of the board needs to be filed down slightly to clear a peripheral channel inside the new case. After reassembly with a new CR2032 battery, the transplanted key worked for both remote locking and keyless start, though the button feel was slightly shallower in the new case.
