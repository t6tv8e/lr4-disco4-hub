## Summary

This video provides a step-by-step guide to replacing the upper tailgate actuator on a Discovery 3/4. The presenter describes it as a relatively easy DIY job. The process involves removing the lower tailgate trim panel, disconnecting the gas strut stays (using a thin 13mm spanner rather than unclipping them), removing the carpet panel, accessing the actuator behind the dust shield, disconnecting the electrical connector and cable, and swapping in a new unit.

The video covers a useful detail about how the cable end connects to the actuator via an L-shaped cable end held under a spring-loaded slug mechanism. When fitting the new actuator, the presenter recommends adding a cable tie around the cable clamp as a precaution, since the plastic clip is known to fail.
