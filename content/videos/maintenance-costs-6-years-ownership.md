## Summary

A candid look at real maintenance costs for a Discovery 4 over 6 years of ownership. The owner bought the car at just over 100,000 miles without any warranty and breaks down spending into phases. The first 3 years cost just £1,400 total (including £600 for Pirelli Scorpion All Terrain tyres and MOT), making it remarkably cheap motoring. Later years saw costs increase as higher-mileage issues emerged. Despite the rising costs, the owner considers it excellent value for what the Discovery 4 offers.

## Notes

- This is one of the few videos providing real long-term cost data rather than speculation
- The early cheap years can create a false sense of security — the bigger bills tend to come later
- No warranty approach worked out overall but requires financial resilience for unexpected repairs
- Torr Overland also has content on D4 off-road modifications
