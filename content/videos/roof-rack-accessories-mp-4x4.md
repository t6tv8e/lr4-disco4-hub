## Summary

Peter corrects his earlier video's criticism that the MP 4x4 roof rack was difficult to mount accessories on, demonstrating spring-loaded sliding nuts that allow completely tool-free insertion and removal anywhere along the rails. He installs sandboard brackets, awning brackets, and large mounting plates, showing the system's modularity and noting the rack's full specifications including the Discovery 4's 100 kg dynamic load limit.
