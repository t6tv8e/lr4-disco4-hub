## Summary

LR TIME completes the final service work on their Discovery 4 after a full engine rebuild, demonstrating that fixing the engine is only the beginning of properly maintaining a neglected vehicle. The video covers a comprehensive ZF automatic transmission fluid change (55,000 km overdue), front and rear differential fluid changes, transfer case fluid, complete rear brake and parking brake service, post-rebuild engine oil inspection, paint correction, and cosmetic restoration. Key technical takeaways include the mandatory transmission adaptation reset procedure, the parking brake electronic bedding-in process, and the critical discovery that front differential fluid degrades faster than rear on road-driven vehicles because the rear diff does all the propulsion work.
