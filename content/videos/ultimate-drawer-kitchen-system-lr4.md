## Summary

Kyle Bell from Air Down Gear Up walks through their SS1 system built for the Land Rover LR4. The system is a modular storage drawer with a full three-stage kitchen, all mounted via machined aluminum anchor bars bolted directly into the vehicle body. The drawer features lock-in/lock-out slides and removable partitions, with a quick-deploying slideout table that works independently of the drawer position.

The three-stage kitchen supports a fridge, stove, and quick-deploying work surface, each operating independently so you can use any combination. The entire system is freestanding -- no legs required -- making it practical for snow, mud, or uneven terrain. A clever spare tire access solution uses the Tough Ant wheel winder adapter, which converts the stock mechanism to a quarter-inch square drive accessible through the center of the structure without disassembling anything.

Additional features include top-access wings for extra storage beside the drawer, and an optional sleeping platform (not equipped on this build).

## Notes

- System is the SS1 model from Air Down Gear Up, their first LR4-specific product
- Freestanding design eliminates leg height adjustment issues on uneven ground
- All three kitchen stages (fridge, stove, work surface) operate independently of each other
- Spare tire access is maintained through a center pass-through point using a quarter-inch extension
- Sleeping platform is an available add-on option
