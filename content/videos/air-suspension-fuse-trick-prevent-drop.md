## Summary

In this short-form video, Peter shares a zero-cost workaround for air suspension sag when the Discovery 4 is parked: open the hood, remove the battery/fuse box cover, and pull the 60 amp fuse. This freezes the suspension at its current set height, preventing the system from deflating overnight. It is a temporary or situational fix and does not address the underlying cause of the air leak.
