## Summary

This video demonstrates how to fit a Powerful UK aftermarket blow-molded rear tailgate spoiler on a Discovery 3/4. The spoiler is available in primer or gloss black and incorporates a later-model Evoque/Range Rover Sport style smoked LED brake light. The kit also includes a replacement washer jet nozzle with an extended hose that routes through the spoiler.

The installation involves removing the upper tailgate trim panel, extracting the existing washer jet, disconnecting the brake light connector, drilling holes for two mechanical fixings using supplied templates, drilling through the existing brake light housing to route the new wire, assembling the spoiler components, trial fitting to check alignment, applying adhesive bond-and-seal, positioning the spoiler, securing with M6 grub screws, clamping the center section during adhesive curing, and making electrical connections using wago-type connectors.
