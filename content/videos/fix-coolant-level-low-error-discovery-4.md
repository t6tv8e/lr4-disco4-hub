## Summary

Peter diagnoses a false "coolant level low" warning on his Discovery 4, finding that the coolant level was consistently correct when physically checked. The cause was a stuck float or faulty sensor inside the coolant expansion tank, a known issue on the Discovery 4. He replaces the entire tank for approximately 40 EUR from Amazon, which involves removing the left headlight for access, clamping hoses to minimize spillage, disconnecting the sensor plug with its safety clip, and refilling with a 1:1 mix of Crystal Team G30 coolant concentrate and water.
