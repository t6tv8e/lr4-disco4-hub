## Summary

This video walks through the removal of the front wheel arch liners on a Discovery 4. The presenters have colour-coded the fasteners on their car to make them easier to identify on camera. The process involves removing a cross-head screw near the headlight area, two pop rivets under the wheel arch at the rear, and potentially an additional self-tapping screw. The headlights need to be removed first, and once all fasteners are out, the arch liner pops off by pulling from the front edge and sliding upward.
