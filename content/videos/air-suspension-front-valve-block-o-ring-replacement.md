## Summary

Peter demonstrates how to fix a front air suspension that struggles to rise by replacing the O-rings inside the front valve block on his Discovery 4. The job requires deflating the system with a GAP IID tool, removing the valve block (one electrical connector and three air line connections), swapping the degraded O-rings, and re-inflating. After the repair, the car successfully held extended mode with no audible leaks, confirming the worn O-rings as the root cause.
