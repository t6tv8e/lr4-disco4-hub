## Summary

Peter, a self-taught Land Rover enthusiast from Austria, explains how he chose the Discovery 4 for its boxy design, interior quality, and off-road capability after rejecting Ford, Toyota, Jeep, the Discovery 5, and the Discovery Sport (boot too small for overlanding). He spent 2-3 years studying workshop manuals and videos before touching the car, motivated by dealership costs exceeding 2,000 euros for a simple brake job. His wife suggested he record his repairs for YouTube, and the channel grew from there.
