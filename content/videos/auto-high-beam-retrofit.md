## Summary

Peter retrofits automatic high beam assist on his Discovery 4 by swapping in a secondhand interior mirror containing the high beam sensor (purchased for around 120 euros versus 450-600 euros new) and activating the feature through two BCM settings in the GAP IID Tool. The entire install takes about 15 minutes, and a night driving test confirms the system correctly engages high beams when no oncoming traffic is detected and switches them off when other vehicles appear.
