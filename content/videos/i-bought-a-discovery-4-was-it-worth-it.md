## Summary

Peter introduces his new project car -- a 2014 Discovery 4 with 257,000 km, one previous owner (a smoker), and a rebuilt engine. The car needs significant work including master brake cylinder replacement, sunroof seal, engine ticking diagnosis, full interior restoration (smoke smell, burned door cards, peeling console), complete exterior paint correction, and all fluid changes. He also outlines plans for a full off-road build with roof rack, rock sliders, snorkel, ladder, and underbody protection.
