## Summary

Peter demonstrates the diesel fuel filter replacement on his Discovery 4, a straightforward 30-minute maintenance task that Land Rover recommends every 12 months or 48,000 km. The job involves removing the underbody protection and heat shield on the passenger side, swapping the Mann filter with new O-rings, aligning the arrow markings for correct orientation, and priming the system by cycling the ignition before starting the engine. Skipping this service risks injector and engine damage from fuel contamination.
