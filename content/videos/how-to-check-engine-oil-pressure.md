## Summary

Peter demonstrates a quick DIY oil pressure test on his TDV6 engine to investigate a persistent ticking noise, using a basic pressure gauge kit with a quarter-inch MPT fitting and extension adapter. Both idle and 3,500 RPM readings came in within Land Rover specifications, definitively ruling out the oil pump as the source of the ticking. During the same session, he also discovered a likely failing alternator showing only 13V charging voltage.
