## Summary

Peter takes his Discovery 4 out for a night drive to test H7 LED headlight bulbs that replaced the factory halogens. The LEDs deliver noticeably brighter, whiter light with impressive side illumination, and produce no CANbus errors. At around 100 euros and road-legal in Austria and Germany (with a compliance form in the glovebox), they represent a practical upgrade over stock halogens for everyday driving.
