## Summary

This video tests whether the new Land Rover Defender L663 18-inch steel wheels can be fitted onto a Discovery 4. The presenter confirms that the Defender 18-inch steel wheels do fit on both front and rear axles, with clearance on both the standard 325mm brakes and the larger 360mm diameter discs. The key to the fitment is the internal profile of the Defender steel wheel, where the inner flange provides just enough clearance (approximately 10mm) over the brake caliper. The video explains why 18-inch wheels are desirable for off-roading: larger sidewall tyres provide better protection for the wheels and better off-road grip, and steel wheels bend rather than crack on impact.
