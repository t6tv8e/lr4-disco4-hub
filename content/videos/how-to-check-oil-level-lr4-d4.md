## Summary

A quick but essential guide showing that the Discovery 4 3.0 TDV6 has no physical oil dipstick and that the oil level must be checked via the dashboard display. The video highlights a common mistake — checking the level immediately after running the engine — and demonstrates the correct procedure. With over 244K views, this is clearly a widespread point of confusion for Discovery 4 owners.
