## Summary

Shane demonstrates the full air suspension recalibration process using the GAP IID diagnostic tool after replacing a rear height sensor and front upper control arms on his Discovery 3. The procedure uses the tool's guided calibration mode, which walks through each corner sequentially -- left front, right front, left rear, right rear. Front corners target 466mm and rear corners target 485mm, measured from the wheel arch to the centre of the wheel. The process requires a level surface, engine running, and a tape measure. After setting all four corners, cycling the suspension up or down once reactivates the system. The presenter notes this procedure is essentially the same across all Land Rover models with air suspension.
