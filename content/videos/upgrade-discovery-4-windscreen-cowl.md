## Summary

Peter replaces the leaking windscreen cowl seal on his Discovery 4 to prevent water from reaching the gearbox PCU located beneath it, which can cause complete loss of gear selection. The OEM Land Rover branded replacement part was sourced from eBay for 140 EUR, less than half the 300+ EUR retail price. The job requires a specialist wiper arm puller tool for corroded spindles, careful marking of wiper positions before removal, and fitting the new clip-mounted seal from the sides inward.
