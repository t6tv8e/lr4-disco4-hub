## Summary

Peter demonstrates the full air suspension calibration process using the GAP IID tool's guided calibration mode. The procedure involves marking the center of each rim with painter's tape, measuring the distance to the wheel arch (466 mm front, 458 mm rear), and using the app's up/down buttons to adjust each corner. The entire process takes about 10 minutes and saves the 200+ euros a Land Rover dealership would charge for the same work.
