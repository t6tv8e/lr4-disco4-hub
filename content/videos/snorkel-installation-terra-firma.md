## Summary

Peter installs an MCH/Terra Firma snorkel on his Discovery 4 as a simpler, cleaner-looking alternative to the Safari snorkel he used on his previous car. The install is straightforward but requires correcting the instructions (9 mm drill bit needed, not 7 mm) and applying Sikaflex sealant before rivet nut insertion to prevent corrosion. The key trade-off is that the Terra Firma snorkel cannot be easily sealed to near-100% like the Safari, making it better suited for dust protection and light water crossings than deep wading.
