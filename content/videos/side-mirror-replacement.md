## Summary

Peter uses the GAP IID Tool to diagnose short-circuit faults in both heated exterior mirrors on his Discovery 4, which had stopped heating in winter. He replaces both mirror glass elements with aftermarket units from eBay for around 60 euros total — versus roughly 500 euros at a Land Rover dealer — using just a screwdriver. A post-repair IID Tool scan confirms zero fault codes, verifying the fix.
