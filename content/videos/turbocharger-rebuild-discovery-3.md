## Summary

LR TIME documents a full turbocharger rebuild on Fabian's Discovery 3 TDV6 (recovered lost footage from a prior engine rebuild project). The original turbocharger had seized completely due to metal chip contamination in the oil from earlier engine damage — the shaft had zero rotation and was locked solid.

Rather than purchasing an OEM replacement at 1500-2500 EUR, a new balanced cartridge was sourced for approximately 130 EUR. The rebuild involves machining the corroded sealing surface flat with a solid carbide cutter and finishing with 320-grit sandpaper to a fingernail-smooth surface, reworking damaged M10 and M8 threads, and critically, transferring the variable geometry vane position setting from the old cartridge to the new one by measuring from a reference locating hole (19.5 mm in this case).

Reassembly requires aligning balancing marks, correctly positioning the dowel pin, orienting the three-dot gasket to match disassembly marks, and applying 1200 degree C high-temp grease. The final essential step is pre-oiling the turbocharger by adding fresh oil directly into the oil inlet port and rotating the shaft by hand before sealing — skipping this will immediately damage the new bearings on first start.

## Notes

- The most critical non-obvious step is accurately transferring the variable geometry vane adjustment — incorrect transfer results in wrong boost behavior
- Replacement cartridge housings can have different geometry than the original, making measurement-transfer essential rather than optional
- Variable geometry vanes (small fins inside the turbo) open/close electronically via actuator and lever to regulate boost — a commonly overlooked failure point
- The actuator connecting rod is known to fail and seize — worth inspecting during any rebuild
- Budget turbocharger cartridges are often cosmetically imperfect reject components from the same casting/manufacturer as OEM
- Reworking threads during the rebuild enables future turbo removal without a body-off job
- Estimated rebuild time: 3+ hours for experienced builders
