## Summary

This video demonstrates the complete process of removing the front bumper from a 2010 (pre-facelift) Discovery 4 and fitting a later 2014 facelift-style bumper. The presenters walk through all the fixings, clips, and electrical connections that need to be disconnected, including parking sensor plugs, fog light wiring, and headlight washer hoses. The video also covers the differences between early and late Discovery 4 tow eye covers, fog light bezels, and washer jets, noting which parts are interchangeable and which need to be replaced when doing an early-to-late bumper conversion.
