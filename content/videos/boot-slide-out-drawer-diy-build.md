## Summary

Peter builds a slide-out drawer for his Discovery 4 boot for around 400 euros, saving over 600 euros compared to the cheapest commercial alternative. The design uses Juncker locking rails rated to 200 kg, water-resistant screen printing plates, and aluminium profiles mounted to the factory boot points. The drawer doubles as a future camping table and preserves under-floor storage, making it practical for a vehicle that also serves as a daily driver.
