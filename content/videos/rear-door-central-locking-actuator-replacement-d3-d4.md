## Summary

This is an updated method for replacing the rear door central locking actuator on a Discovery 3/4. The presenters previously covered this topic but have developed a better technique that involves removing the outer door handle rather than working blind inside the door to swap cables. While removing more parts, they argue this approach is actually easier and produces better results.

The full sequence covered is: remove the door card, drop the window, remove the speaker (4x T20 Torx screws), disengage the window from the regulator mechanism (rear window has only one clip vs two on the front), remove the window regulator assembly, remove the exterior door handle and its cover trim, unbolt the lock mechanism (3x T30 Torx), extract the whole assembly, then disconnect the cables from the old actuator and transfer them to the new one.
