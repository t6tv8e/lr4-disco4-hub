## Summary

Peter tests the CarPurite portable 10-inch CarPlay/Android Auto screen on his Discovery 4, highlighting its wireless connectivity, fast boot time, and FM transmitter for older vehicles without modern audio inputs. The standout tip is a dual-Bluetooth hack that connects the phone to both the car's native system and the portable unit simultaneously, enabling steering wheel media controls to work with Spotify on the external screen.
