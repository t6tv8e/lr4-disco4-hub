## Summary

Peter provides real-world performance data for his Lensun 110W solar hood panel after 11 days of use, showing 70W output in direct sun and 36W on cloudy days, with 510 Wh total generated. The free Solar Life app requires no registration and monitors production, battery status, and historical data via Bluetooth. He emphasises the value for Land Rover owners specifically, since low battery voltage triggers cascading dashboard error codes -- making passive solar charging a practical preventive measure.
