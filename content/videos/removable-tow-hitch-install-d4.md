## Summary

This video demonstrates how to install the removable tow hitch (tow ball) on a Land Rover Discovery 3/4 or Range Rover Sport. These vehicles come with a tow bar already built into the rear chassis -- you only need to purchase the removable tow hitch assembly itself. The video covers the locking mechanism, insertion procedure, and common difficulties encountered when fitting the hitch into the chassis receiver.

The presenters also discuss an important safety recall related to tow bar vibration causing chassis wear, and show the difference between the standard removable hitch and a heavy-duty version designed to be left installed permanently.
