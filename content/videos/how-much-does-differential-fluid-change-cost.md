## Summary

Peter demonstrates a rear differential oil change on his Range Rover Evoque, a beginner-friendly job costing under 20 EUR in materials and taking less than 30 minutes. The magnetic drain plug revealed significant metal particle accumulation, providing clear visual evidence of why this often-neglected service matters. While this video features the Evoque rather than the Discovery 4, the principles and technique are directly applicable to Land Rover differential maintenance.
