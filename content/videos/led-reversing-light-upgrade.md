## Summary

Peter upgrades the dim factory reversing lights on his Discovery 4 to LED bulbs purchased from Amazon for around 15 euros. The swap takes about 10 minutes per side with just a screwdriver, and the LED bulbs are CANbus-compatible so they trigger no dashboard errors. A night-time comparison shows a dramatic improvement in brightness, with the LEDs illuminating the entire driveway.
