## Summary

Peter retrofits an OEM-style cooler/fridge unit into his Discovery 4 center console, replacing the standard storage box. The upgrade is essentially plug-and-play because the Discovery 4 already has factory wiring pre-run for the cooler unit. The installation involves removing the armrest assembly with T40 and T20 Torx bits, swapping the storage box for the cooler, and connecting the existing cable -- with the cooler confirmed working immediately after engine start.
