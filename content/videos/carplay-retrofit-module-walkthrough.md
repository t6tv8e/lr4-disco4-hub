## Summary

Peter demonstrates his 290-euro retrofit CarPlay/Android Auto module on the Discovery 4, correcting his earlier claim that Android Auto requires a wired connection — it works wirelessly. The module replaces the outdated factory navigation but preserves all original vehicle functions, with the main trade-off being the loss of steering wheel media controls for track skipping, which would require a significantly more expensive alternative module.
