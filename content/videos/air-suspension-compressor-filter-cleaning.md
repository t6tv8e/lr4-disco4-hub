## Summary

Peter shows how to service three filters on the Discovery 4, with the highlight being a little-known air suspension compressor intake filter hidden in the left side of the trunk. This foam sponge element sits inside a small plastic cylinder connected to the air suspension tubing and can be cleaned with warm water and soap. The video also covers the engine bay air filter (7 screws, can be blown out with a compressor mid-season) and the cabin pollen filter behind the lower glove box (tool-free swap, Mann Filter brand, airflow arrows must point down).
