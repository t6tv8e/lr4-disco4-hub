## Summary

Peter replaces the worn and malfunctioning left-side steering wheel control panel on his Discovery 4, which had degraded buttons and an unreliable volume control. The job requires safely removing the airbag using a specialist tool (available for 6-7 EUR), disconnecting the battery for at least 10 minutes beforehand, then accessing the control panel secured by two T20 Torx screws. The trickiest step is routing the very short bottom cable during reinstallation, which Peter solves by using the airbag tool to gain 2-3 cm of extra slack.
