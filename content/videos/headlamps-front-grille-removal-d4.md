## Summary

This video demonstrates how to remove the front grille and headlamps on a Discovery 4 as the first step in a pre-facelift to facelift conversion project. The grille removal is straightforward -- spring-loaded tabs along the top and ball-end clips along the bottom. The headlamp removal involves sliding two retaining tabs and disconnecting a 16-way electrical connector from each headlamp.

The presenters work through the process live without prior practice, which helpfully reveals the real-world difficulties. One headlamp came out easily while the other was very tight, requiring wiggling and pushing the headlamp in before pulling it out. They note that the ball-end grille clips can deteriorate after repeated removal and are worth replacing.
