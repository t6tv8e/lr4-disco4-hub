## Summary

Peter provides a comprehensive build update on his Discovery 4, covering completed exterior mods (MP 4x4 roof rack, rock sliders, underbody protection, rear ladder), interior restoration (seat cushions, LED conversion, climate cleaning), and critical mechanical work. The most significant finding is a workshop diagnosis attributing the persistent engine ticking to a leaking copper sealing ring between the injector and the engine block rather than a worn timing chain, since the noise only appears after the engine warms up. A full gearbox service also resolved previously harsh shifting.
