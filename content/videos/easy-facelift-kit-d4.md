## Summary

This video walks through a complete front-end facelift conversion to make an early (pre-facelift) Discovery 4 look like the later facelift model, without requiring any paintwork or body shop visits. The presenter explains the key visual differences between early and late D4s: round vs square fog light surrounds, orange-accented headlights vs blacked-out headlights with signature LED strip, and the different grille shape (straighter on early, more curved on late).

The kit approach uses a custom injection-moulded filler strip to bridge the gap between the late grille and the early bumper, plus clip-on "fake" fog light bezels that replicate the recessed late-model look. The headlights are plug-and-play but must match the existing type (halogen to halogen, xenon to xenon). The entire conversion can be completed in a Saturday morning with basic tools.
