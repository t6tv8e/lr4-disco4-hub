## Summary

Christian diagnoses and fixes three issues to pass the German TUV vehicle inspection on their Discovery 3. The most involved repair was an airbag warning light caused by fault code B0091 (Left Side Restraint Sensor), traced to a corroded connector between the driver's door pillar and door body. The diagnostic clue was simple: the fault appeared when the door was opened and cleared when closed. The fix required removing the door from its hinges, cleaning the connector with contact cleaner, and sealing it with silicone to prevent future water ingress. A forgotten brake pad wear sensor from a previous wheel bearing job was also replaced, and two licence plate bulbs were swapped. The vehicle passed TUV inspection after all repairs.
