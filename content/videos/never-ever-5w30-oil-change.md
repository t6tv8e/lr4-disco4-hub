## Summary

Peter walks through a complete DIY oil change on his Discovery 4, switching from the Land Rover recommended Fuchs 5W-30 (C2) to Castrol 5W-40 (C3) for better engine protection. The video covers Discovery-specific quirks including the mandatory air suspension lockout before lifting, the awkwardly positioned drain plug that splashes oil onto the chassis, and the unusual digital-only oil level check procedure that requires running the engine for 10 minutes followed by a 10-minute rest period.
