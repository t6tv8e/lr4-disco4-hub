## Summary

Peter removes all seven seats from his Discovery 4 to deep-clean the carpet and eliminate cigarette smoke odor left by the previous owner. The video covers each row methodically: front seats secured by four bolts with hidden electrical connectors, second-row seats that must be removed in a specific order (outer seats first), and fragile third-row seats with easily breakable plastic covers. Key tips include lifting seats straight up off locating pins, using a T50 Torx for seatbelt anchors, and taking extra care with clips in cold weather.
