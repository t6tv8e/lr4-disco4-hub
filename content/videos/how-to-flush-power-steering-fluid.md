## Summary

Peter demonstrates a full power steering fluid flush and expansion tank replacement on his Discovery 4, a service Land Rover recommends every 2 years or 48,000 km. The procedure involves replacing the expansion tank (which has a non-serviceable integrated filter), redirecting the return hose into a collection canister, and flushing 4 liters of Liqui Moly ATF through the system while a helper turns the steering wheel lock-to-lock. Total parts cost runs approximately 90-100 EUR, and the key practical lesson is to remove any funnel strainer during flushing to avoid starving the power steering pump.
