## Summary

PlasmaDave presents a candid 4.5-year ownership report on a 2010 Discovery 4 SE in Australia. The video covers a substantial repair history including air suspension failures (compressor and airbags), alternator failure, cracked throttle body, oil cooler failure, cracked inlet manifold, and several smaller issues. The owner's approach is entirely DIY, with frequent oil changes (every 5,000 km) as the primary strategy against the known crankshaft failure risk. At a current value of roughly AUD $17,000, the case is made that dealer repairs are economically unjustifiable and DIY is the only viable ownership path.

## Notes

- This is one of the most detailed single-owner repair histories available for the D4
- The oil cooler + inlet manifold lesson is valuable: if doing one, plan to do both while the engine is apart
- 7 seats, rear A/C, cloth interior, no electric seats configuration
- Australian market vehicle
