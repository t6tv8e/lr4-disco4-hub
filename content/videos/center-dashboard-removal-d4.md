## Summary

This video shows how to remove the centre dashboard side panels and trim on a Discovery 4, following on from a lower console removal video. The process covers removing the trim panel below the screen, the silver and grey fascia panels, the side cheek panels, and also demonstrates how to remove the start/stop switch. The presenters note that all screws in this job are the same size (T20 Torx), making it straightforward.

The video is relatively short and serves as a middle step in a series -- the lower console has already been removed, and a follow-up video covers removing the screen and head unit. A useful bonus tip is included: the start/stop switch can be replaced by just levering it out with a trim tool, without needing to remove any other dashboard components.
