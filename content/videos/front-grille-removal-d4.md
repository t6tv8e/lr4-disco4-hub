## Summary

A very short and straightforward video showing how to remove and refit the front grille on a Discovery 4. The process requires no tools — just four clips at the top and two prong/docking-station locators at the bottom. The grille is often removed as a prerequisite for other front-end work such as bumper removal or headlight access.
