## Summary

This video provides a detailed side-by-side comparison of the 2020 Land Rover Defender 110 (L663) and the Discovery 4. The presenter parks both vehicles nose-to-nose and walks around them, comparing styling, dimensions, interior space, load capacity, and specifications. While the vehicles are nearly identical in length, width, and performance, the Discovery 4 has a significantly larger boot space (110cm floor vs 87cm in the Defender) and can fit a standard pallet with rear seats folded. The Defender is lighter by 300kg thanks to its aluminium monocoque construction vs the D4's body-on-chassis design. The Defender wins on interior technology and infotainment, while the D4 excels in practical load space and the versatility of its split tailgate design.
