## Summary

Peter provides an honest five-pros-three-cons review of the GAP IID Tool for Land Rover Discovery 4 ownership. The pros centre on significant cost savings versus dealer visits, smartphone-based portability, responsive customer support, the ability to activate hidden vehicle features, and broad vehicle compatibility. The cons include the roughly 470 euro upfront price, a 70 euro per-vehicle licensing fee for additional cars, and paid ECU updates at 30 euros each. A live walkthrough demonstrates fault reading, height adjustment, guided air suspension calibration, live sensor data, ECU configuration, and the save/restore backup function.
