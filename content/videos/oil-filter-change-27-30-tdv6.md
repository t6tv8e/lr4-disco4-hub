## Summary

A practical walkthrough of the Discovery 4 oil and filter change, emphasising that these engines should have their oil extracted via pump rather than draining through the sump plug. The presenter demonstrates the full process using a budget lubricant pump purchased for around $30-40 AUD, making it an accessible DIY job.

## Notes

- The video covers both 2.7 TDV6 and 3.0 SDV6 engines as the process is essentially the same
- The pump extraction method is presented as the correct way to service these vehicles, contrary to the traditional sump plug drain used on most cars
