## H7 LED Bulb Upgrade: Real-World Night Driving Review

*TLDR: A Land Rover Discovery 4 owner tests aftermarket H7 LED bulbs in real night-driving conditions, finding them a worthwhile upgrade over standard halogens at a reasonable price point.*

---

### 🔦 Performance Observations

- **Brightness**: LEDs are significantly brighter than standard halogen H7 bulbs, with a crisp white color output versus the yellow tint of halogens.
- **Side illumination**: A standout strength is strong lateral light spread, functioning similarly to fog lights and improving visibility of roadside hazards.
- **Long-distance performance**: Adequate for general driving but not comparable to premium **matrix LED or laser headlight systems**.

---

- **Cost**: Approximately **€100**, considered good value for the performance improvement offered.

- **Canbus compatibility**: No Canbus errors triggered, meaning no dashboard warning lights or additional programming required.

---

- **Tools required**: Only a screwdriver — no specialist equipment needed.
