## DIY Engine Smoothness: Diesel Additive & Viscosity Oil Upgrade for a Land Rover

*TLDR: A Land Rover owner shares hands-on results from using a Liqui Moly diesel additive and switching to a higher-viscosity oil to fix cold-start jerking and engine noise.*

---

### 🔧 The Problem Being Solved

- **Cold-start jerking**: In winter, the engine would violently shake left and right when started without pre-heating — a common symptom of dirty or underperforming injectors.
- **Engine noise on cold start**: The engine produced a loud "nailing" sound at first ignition, pointing to inadequate lubrication at startup.

---

### 🧪 Product 1: Liqui Moly Diesel Additive (~€10–15)

- **Cleans injectors**: A referenced YouTube test showed one injector only dripping fuel before treatment; after ~**500 km** with the additive, all injectors sprayed correctly.
- **Improves exhaust gas values**: Recommended for anyone failing emissions tests — drive ~500 km after adding it and values improve measurably.
- **Better cold ignition**: Improves the diesel's ignition properties, directly addressing cold-start performance issues.
- **Prevents and cleans soot deposits** on cylinder walls.
- **Personal result**: Jerking disappeared after **300–400 km** on the first use and never returned; now used every **2,000 km** as maintenance.
- **Works best on high-mileage vehicles**: Cars under **100,000 km** may notice little difference; cars over **100,000–200,000 km** will see the most benefit due to accumulated carbon deposits.
- **Recommended dosing**: Add **two cans back-to-back** (each near an empty tank, then fill with diesel) for an initial deep clean.

---

### 🛢️ Product 2: Castrol Magnetic 5W-40 Oil (replacing Land Rover's recommended 5W-30)

- **Higher viscosity (5W-40 vs. 5W-30)** provides a thicker oil film, especially beneficial for older or higher-mileage engines.
- **Immediate noise reduction**: On the very first cold start after the oil change, the engine nailing was noticeably quieter — not eliminated but significantly reduced.
- **No fuel consumption penalty**: Despite the higher viscosity (which theoretically increases resistance), diesel consumption remained unchanged.
- **~1,500 km** of use reported with consistently smoother engine behavior.

---

### 📋 Key Takeaways & Recommendations

- **Combine both products** for best results: the additive cleans the fuel system while the higher-viscosity oil improves mechanical lubrication.
- Both products are **low-cost, low-risk interventions** — the additive costs €10–15, and the oil swap requires no mechanical expertise.
- Neither product is sponsored — recommendations are based purely on personal experience.

---

### 💡 Bottom Line

For owners of **older or high-mileage diesel vehicles** (especially Land Rovers) experiencing rough cold starts or engine noise, switching to a **5W-40 oil** and running a **Liqui Moly diesel additive** every 2,000 km is a cheap, practical fix that avoids more expensive diagnostics or repairs. The combination targets two root causes simultaneously: dirty injectors and insufficient oil film at startup.
