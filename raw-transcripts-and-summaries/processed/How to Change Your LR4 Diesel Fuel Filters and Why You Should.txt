## DIY Diesel Filter Replacement on a Land Rover Discovery 4

*A step-by-step guide to replacing the diesel fuel filter on a Land Rover Discovery 4, completable in about 30 minutes.*

---

### 🔧 Why Replace the Fuel Filter

- **Contamination risk**: Over time, particles and dirt accumulate in diesel fuel; a clogged filter can allow contaminants to reach injectors or the engine, causing potentially serious damage.
- **Recommended interval**: Land Rover specifies replacement every **12 months or 48,000 km** — whichever comes first.
- **This service was overdue**: The filter in the video had been in service for **16–18 months**, making replacement urgent.

---

### 🛒 Parts & Preparation

- **Recommended filter**: Mann filter (brand used in video), sourced from Amazon for approximately **€70–80**; a link is provided in the video description.
- **New O-rings included**: The Mann filter kit includes new orange O-rings, which **must be replaced** — there are two (one on the drain plug, one on the sensor).
- **Setup required**: Raise the car as high as possible, position jack stands, and place a **fuel catch container** beneath the filter before starting.

---

### 🔩 Step-by-Step Replacement Process

1. **Remove underbody protection panel** using **10mm bolts** to gain access to the filter area on the **passenger side**.
2. **Remove the heat shield** (three bolts) covering the diesel filter.
3. **Disconnect the sensor connector** on the filter.
4. **Unscrew the filter anti-clockwise** and remove it into the catch container — expect some fuel spillage.
5. **Transfer components**: Remove the drain plug and sensor from the old filter; clean them and swap the **two O-rings** with the new ones included in the kit.
6. **Prepare the new filter**: Reattach the drain plug and sensor; note the **arrow markings** on the filter body indicating correct orientation.
7. **Reinstall**: Align the arrow on the filter with the unlock marker, push in, and rotate to the **locked position**.
8. **Reconnect the sensor connector**, then refit the heat shield and underbody protection.

---

### ⚡ Priming & Starting the System

- **Do not crank the engine immediately** after installation — the empty filter must be primed first.
- **Prime method**: Turn the ignition to the **ON position (without starting)**; the fuel pump will audibly activate and fill the filter. Repeat the cycle **twice** until the priming sound stops.
- **Start the engine** and inspect the underbody for any diesel leaks before considering the job complete.

---

### 💡 Bottom Line

Replacing the diesel filter on a Land Rover Discovery 4 is straightforward **DIY maintenance** requiring roughly **30 minutes and ~€70–80 in parts**. Skipping this service risks injector and engine damage from fuel contamination — making it one of the most cost-effective preventive tasks an owner can perform themselves.
