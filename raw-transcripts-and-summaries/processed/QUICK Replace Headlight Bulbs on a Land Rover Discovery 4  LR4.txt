## How to Change Headlight Bulbs on a Land Rover Discovery 4

*TLDR: A straightforward, tool-free guide to replacing H7 headlight bulbs on a Land Rover Discovery 4, using Osram Night Breaker 150%+ bulbs.*

---

### 🔦 What's Being Replaced & Why

- **Yellowing OEM bulbs**: The stock headlight bulbs had turned noticeably yellow and were showing signs of failure (blackening inside the bulb), prompting an early replacement.
- **Full lighting refresh**: The owner also previously replaced the xenons (with bluer units) and fog lights, making this H7 high-beam swap the final step.
- **Chosen replacement**: **Osram Night Breaker 150%+** H7 bulbs, rated for up to **150 meters** of illumination range and a whiter/cooler color output (~5000–6000K).

---

### 🛠️ Tools Required

- **No tools needed** for the main disassembly (grille and headlight unit removal are fully clip-based).
- A **screwdriver** is optionally helpful for releasing the small wire clip holding the bulb in place — long fingernails can substitute.

---

### 📋 Step-by-Step Process

- **Step 1 – Open the hood**: Required to access the clip release points for the front grille from above.
- **Step 2 – Remove the grille**: Push the plastic clips downward/upward to release, then pull the grille outward firmly — it requires a bit of force but no tools.
- **Step 3 – Release the headlight unit**: Two plastic locking tabs (one visible through a hole in the housing) must be pushed upward to unlock; the entire headlight assembly then pulls straight out.
- **Step 4 – Disconnect the electrical plug**: A small clip on top of the connector must be pressed before the plug can be pulled free.
- **Step 5 – Access the H7 bulb**: Open the rear cover of the headlight unit, disconnect the bulb's connector, then press the wire retaining clip to release and remove the bulb.
- **Step 6 – Install new bulb**: Push the new H7 bulb into the socket and secure the retaining clip — it clicks into place without tools.
- **Step 7 – Reconnect and reassemble**: Plug the connector back in, reinsert the headlight unit (align tabs), and clip the grille back into place.

---

### ⚠️ Notes & Caveats

- **Result was underwhelming**: The Osram Night Breaker bulbs were **not as white as expected**, prompting the creator to order **Philips H7 LED replacements** instead.
- **LED legality**: The upcoming Philips H7 LED bulbs are confirmed **road-legal in Austria and Germany** — worth verifying for other regions before purchasing.
- **Additional accessories**: The creator had aftermarket LED high-beam lights mounted in the grille area, requiring extra removal steps not relevant to a stock vehicle.

---

### 💡 Bottom Line

This procedure is genuinely beginner-friendly — the Discovery 4's clip-based headlight design means anyone can swap H7 bulbs in under 30 minutes with minimal tools. The key takeaway beyond the how-to is the honest product feedback: if you want noticeably whiter output, **skip the Osram Night Breaker and go straight to a legal LED H7 option** like the Philips upgrade shown in the follow-up video.
