- **Cable routing**: The power cable runs from the panel down through foam insulation along the hood, secured with **3M adhesive cable mounts and cable ties**.

- **Grounding**: Earth/minus cables are connected to a **chassis ground point on the outside of the car** (avoiding overcrowded OEM ground points).
- **Plus cable routing**: The positive cable runs under the headliner to the front, terminating with an **inline fuse near the battery** to protect the circuit.
- **Auxiliary battery support**: The charge controller can prioritize charging an **auxiliary (e.g., lithium-ion) battery first**, then top up the starter battery.

- **Historical data**: Shows a **day-by-day power production log**, total running days, and cumulative energy generated.
