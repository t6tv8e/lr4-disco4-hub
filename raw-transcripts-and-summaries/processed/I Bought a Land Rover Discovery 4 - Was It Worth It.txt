## New Land Rover Discovery 4 Project: A Rough Fixer-Upper Introduced

*A YouTube creator sells his 2012 Discovery 4 and introduces a heavily worn 2014 replacement that needs extensive cosmetic, mechanical, and interior restoration.*

---

### 🚗 The New Acquisition

- **Why a new Discovery 4**: The creator sold his 2012 black Discovery 4 (with rooftop tent, water tank, and slide-out) because it went unused, then impulsively decided "life is too short not to drive a Land Rover" and bought a replacement two weeks later.
- **Key specs**: **2014 model year**, **257,000 km** on the odometer, engine previously rebuilt at approximately **80,000 km**, and notably only **one previous owner** — a smoker.
- **Upgrade motivation**: The new car is a **7-seater**, which the creator specifically wanted for extra carrying capacity.

---

### 🔧 Mechanical Issues & Work Already Done

- **Completed**: Changed oil (5W-40 C3), oil filter, air filter, and pollen filter — engine now runs smoothly; front and rear brakes replaced.
- **Clicking engine noise**: An unidentified clicking sound from the engine bay needs professional workshop diagnosis.
- **Soft brake pedal**: The brake pedal travels all the way to the floor, indicating a **failed master brake cylinder** that must be replaced; brake fluid will also be flushed.
- **Upcoming fluid changes**: All differential oils and coolant scheduled for replacement; diesel filter still pending.
- **Sunroof seal**: The current seal allows water ingress and must be replaced.

---

### 🪑 Interior Restoration Needed

- **Smell and cleanliness**: The cabin smells of smoke — all seats will be removed and carpets deep-cleaned.
- **Damaged surfaces**: Door cards have burn marks, the center console paint is peeling, and the old wood trim will be fully replaced.
- **Seat replacement**: Driver and passenger seats are visibly worn and will be swapped out entirely.
- **Steering wheel**: Paint/finish is scratched; creator is considering a full steering wheel replacement.
- **Tech upgrades planned**: **Apple CarPlay** integration and an **X-lifter** installation are on the to-do list.

---

### 🎨 Exterior Restoration Planned

- **Paint damage**: Scratches and missing paint are present across the front bumper, door sills, and body panels — a full **respray/paint correction** is needed.
- **Entry area damage**: The driver-side door sill and entry area are heavily scuffed and damaged.
- **Wheels**: Summer rims (carried over from the old black Discovery) are in poor condition and need **repainting**; final tire/wheel choice is still undecided.

---

### 🏕️ Off-Road Build Goals

- **Planned accessories**: Roof rack, rock sliders, rear ladder, underbody protection plates, **safari snorkel**, off-road exhausts, and LED fog lights.
- **Aesthetic direction**: The build is clearly aimed at a capable overlanding/off-road setup, continuing the channel's adventure vehicle theme.
